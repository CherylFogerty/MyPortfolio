About Me

My name is Cheryl Fogerty welcome to my page! For 22 years I worked as a Broadcast Technician for KSDK, a local tv station in St. Louis MO until I was laid off in 2020. During my tenure at KSDK I worked in every aspect of the Broadcast industry from production to operations. Upon being laid off I decided to make a career change and learn computer coding and web design. This page is my first attempt at a new start, I hope you enjoy the content as much as I enjoyed making it. Thank you for stopping by.
